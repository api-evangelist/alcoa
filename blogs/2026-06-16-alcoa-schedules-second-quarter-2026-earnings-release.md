Alcoa Corporation will release its second quarter 2026 financial results on Thursday, July 16, 2026, after the NYSE close, and host a conference call the same day at 5:00 p.m. EDT (7:00 a.m. AEST on July 17). President and CEO William Oplinger and CFO Molly Beerman will lead the webcast, with a telephone replay available through July 23, 2026.
