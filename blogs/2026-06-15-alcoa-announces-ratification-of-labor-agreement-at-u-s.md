Alcoa Corporation ratified a new four-year labor agreement with the United Steelworkers union covering approximately 965 employees at its Warrick Operations in Indiana and Massena Operations in New York. The agreement runs from May 16, 2026 through May 15, 2030 and was ratified by a wide margin.
